Dream In Cube

Navigate each cube, push boxes, ride the ice, find the stairs.

Use W/A/S/D or the arrow keys to move.

Cube Overview

0
Wall
#
Pushable box
+
Ice: slide until blocked
^
Stairs to next cube

How to Play

Move the cube explorer with W/A/S/D (or the arrow keys). The view always follows the face you are standing on, and the minimap shows how the cube is oriented. Push boxes to clear paths, ride over ice until something stops you, and step on the stairs to reach the next cube.

Levels are loaded directly from the original Dream In Cube data files. Reach the end of all cubes to win.
